SOHO INTERNATIONAL FOOD TOURS

Eat, Drink, and enjoy a cultural walking tour of Soho!

Our Soho International Food Tour is an exploration of Soho, one of London’s most diverse and historically rich neighbourhoods. Bordering Oxford and Regent Street, this small borough is always at the forefront in trends, and one thing never lacking, is food. From the traditional to the some of the newest fusions in the city, Soho is the place to learn about London’s incredible International Food scene.

Our 4.5 tour will take you through some of Soho’s most charming streets, as you taste a wide variety of International Foods from some of our favorite places in Soho. Read More or Book Now
